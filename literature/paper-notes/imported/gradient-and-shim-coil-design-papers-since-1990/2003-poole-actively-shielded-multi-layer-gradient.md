## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The multilayer gradient concept is extended to actively shielded coils with improved cooling and resistance behavior. The design addresses the strong-gradient problem in small-bore MRI, where single-layer coils become resistance-limited. The work links electromagnetic optimization with practical thermal and winding constraints.
