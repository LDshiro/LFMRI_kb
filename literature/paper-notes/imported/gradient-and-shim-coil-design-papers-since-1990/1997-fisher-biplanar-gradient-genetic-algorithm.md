## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The authors design a biplanar z-gradient coil by genetic algorithm optimization and compare its efficiency with a conventional Maxwell arrangement. The design is evaluated over a cuboid optimized region, and additional data are provided for transverse gradient coils. The paper illustrates how evolutionary optimization can handle unconventional biplanar gradient layouts.
