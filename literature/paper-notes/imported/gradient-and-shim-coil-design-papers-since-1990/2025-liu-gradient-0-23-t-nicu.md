## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper targets a compact neonatal intensive care unit MRI system at 0.23 T. It uses a two-step target-field method: a Tikhonov-regularized linear solve followed by nonlinear constrained optimization. The goal is to improve absolute and relative gradient linearity while accommodating compact geometry and finite gradient thickness.
