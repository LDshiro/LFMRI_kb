## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The paper applies simulated annealing to the direct design of biplanar gradient coils for NMR microscopy. Instead of first solving for a continuous stream function, it optimizes wire positions directly with respect to homogeneity, efficiency, and power-related criteria. The study demonstrates the method for biplanar x- and z-gradient coils.
