## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The authors combine a target-field point method with stream-function theory to design biplanar gradient coils. Inverse simulation is used to obtain a winding distribution, followed by solid modeling and orthogonal analysis. The design is iteratively adjusted for linearity, efficiency, and uniformity within the desired spherical volume.
