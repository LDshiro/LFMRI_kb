## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper combines stream functions with the target-field method to derive complicated shim-coil winding patterns. It is aimed at cases where desired field shapes and finite geometry make traditional simple harmonic windings inadequate. The stream-function representation provides a direct route from optimized surface current density to manufacturable contours.
