## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

Minimum-inductance design can strongly reduce stored magnetic energy, but planar gradient coils obtained this way may have poor field linearity. This paper improves planar-coil linearity by scaling spatial frequencies in the current-density function. The result is a better figure of merit with only a small penalty in inductance.
