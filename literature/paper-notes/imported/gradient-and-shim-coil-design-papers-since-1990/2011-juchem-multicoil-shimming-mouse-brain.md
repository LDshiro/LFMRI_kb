## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

A 48-coil local shim array is used for static and dynamic shimming of the mouse brain at high field. The multi-coil basis reduces residual B0 distortions more effectively than conventional second-order spherical-harmonic shimming. The paper demonstrates improved gradient-echo signal recovery and localized correction flexibility.
