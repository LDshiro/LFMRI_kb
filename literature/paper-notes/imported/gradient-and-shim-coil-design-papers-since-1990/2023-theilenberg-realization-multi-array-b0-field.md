## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This work designs and realizes a 31-channel multi-coil array for B0 field generation, image encoding, and advanced shimming in a compact 1.5 T head-only scanner. The design is evaluated in simulation and bench tests, including gradient strength, cooling, and duty-cycle considerations. It demonstrates how multi-coil hardware can blur the line between shimming and encoding.
