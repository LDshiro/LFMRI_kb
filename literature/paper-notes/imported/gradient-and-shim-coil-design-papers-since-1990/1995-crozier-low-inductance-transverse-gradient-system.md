## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

A restricted-length transverse gradient assembly is designed to provide a large usable region while keeping inductance low. The authors present a prototype and compare measured behavior with simulations. The design targets applications that require fast switching, such as head, animal, or microimaging MRI/NMR systems.
