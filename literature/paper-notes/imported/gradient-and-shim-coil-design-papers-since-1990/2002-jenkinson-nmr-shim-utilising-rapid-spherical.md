## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The paper uses a rapid spherical-harmonic calculation method to design shim coils from circular-arc conductors. Simulated annealing is used to locate current-carrying arcs so that undesired harmonic terms are minimized while the desired shim term remains. The method is flexible for both zonal and tesseral fields.
