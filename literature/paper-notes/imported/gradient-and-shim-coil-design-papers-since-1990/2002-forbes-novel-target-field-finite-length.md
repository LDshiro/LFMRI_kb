## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The second paper extends the finite-length target-field method to tesseral shim fields. It avoids assuming an ideal infinitely long geometry and allows asymmetric target regions and fields specified on more than one radius. The method provides winding patterns for higher-order tesseral shim terms in realistic finite coils.
