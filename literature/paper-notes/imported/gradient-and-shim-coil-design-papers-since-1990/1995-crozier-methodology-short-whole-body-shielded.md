## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper uses real-space simulated annealing to design short whole-body shielded gradient coils. It emphasizes restricted-length systems, remote return paths, and the need to maintain a useful homogeneous gradient region while reducing external fields. The approach is relevant for compact or short-bore gradient systems.
