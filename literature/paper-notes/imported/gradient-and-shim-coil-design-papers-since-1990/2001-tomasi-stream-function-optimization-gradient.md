## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This work parameterizes cylindrical gradient-coil current density by stream functions and optimizes them, combining target-field ideas with simulated annealing. It targets short self-shielded cylindrical gradients and compares performance with conventional target-field designs. The method can enlarge the homogeneous volume and reduce inductance for selected cases.
