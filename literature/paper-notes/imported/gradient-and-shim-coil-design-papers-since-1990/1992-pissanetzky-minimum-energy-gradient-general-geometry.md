## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

A general minimum-energy method is proposed for MRI gradient coils on arbitrary geometries. The formulation permits constraints such as finite cylinder length, finite planar support, current-free slots, eddy-current effects, and shielded or double-shielded structures. The optimization minimizes magnetic energy after these effects are included, yielding lower-energy and lower-inductance designs than comparable previous approaches.
