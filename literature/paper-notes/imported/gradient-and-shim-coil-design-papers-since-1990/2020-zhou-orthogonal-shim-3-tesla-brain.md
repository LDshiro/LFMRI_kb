## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper proposes an orthogonal shim-coil array for 3 T brain imaging, with coil planes arranged to reduce interaction with neighboring RF coils. Simulation and experiment show improved B0 homogeneity while limiting RF-performance penalties. It is an example of shim-array design under simultaneous electromagnetic and receive-array constraints.
