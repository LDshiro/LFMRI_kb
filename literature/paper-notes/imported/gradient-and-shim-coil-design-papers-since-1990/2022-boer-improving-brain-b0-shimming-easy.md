## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The paper presents a relatively simple multi-coil shim array for ultra-high-field brain MRI. The design aims at accessibility and practical construction rather than maximum channel count. Static and slice-based shimming tests show measurable improvements over conventional second-order shimming.
