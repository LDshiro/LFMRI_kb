## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This review organizes MRI gradient-coil design methods into discrete-wire and continuous-current-density approaches. It discusses field linearity, inductance, power dissipation, efficiency, low fringe fields, and active shielding. The paper is a useful map of the target-field, constrained-inductance, and constrained-power design literature up to the early 1990s.
