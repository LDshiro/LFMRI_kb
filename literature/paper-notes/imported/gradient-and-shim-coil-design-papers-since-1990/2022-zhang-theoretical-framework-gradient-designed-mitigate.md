## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The authors develop a planar-gradient design framework for permanent-magnet MRI systems where active shielding may be geometrically difficult. A mirror-image magnetostatic model and finite-difference stream-function formulation are used to reduce eddy currents in conductive plates and pole pieces. The method aims at minimum power dissipation and magnetic energy under practical spacing constraints.
