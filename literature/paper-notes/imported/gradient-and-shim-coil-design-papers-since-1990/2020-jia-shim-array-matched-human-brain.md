## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The authors design multi-channel shim coil arrays whose geometry is matched to human brain anatomy and measured B0 field-map statistics. Stream-function methods and singular-value decomposition are used with cross-validation over many subjects and head positions. The resulting arrays outperform high-order spherical-harmonic shims with fewer, anatomically optimized elements.
