## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper designs first- and second-order biplanar active shim coils for a 50 mT ultra-low-field MRI system. The measured main field is expanded in orthogonal spherical harmonics, and harmonic coefficients guide the required shim terms. The work is directly relevant to low-field systems where magnet homogeneity strongly affects signal-to-noise ratio.
