## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This methods paper provides an intuitive and open-source-oriented route from surface current density to stream functions and thin-wire gradient windings. It demonstrates how inverse coil design can be implemented on simple surfaces and several topologies. The value is mainly pedagogical and reproducibility-oriented for stream-function based gradient design.
