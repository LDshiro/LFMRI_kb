## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper models magnetic fields generated by many independent localized coils. The same basis of individual coil fields can synthesize shim fields and, in some cases, spatial-encoding gradients. The study anticipates multi-coil field control, where the effective volume and correction field can be adapted by optimizing currents rather than by relying only on fixed spherical-harmonic hardware.
