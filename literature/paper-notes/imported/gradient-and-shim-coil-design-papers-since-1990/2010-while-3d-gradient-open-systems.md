## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

An analytic inverse method is presented for obtaining three-dimensional gradient coil geometries appropriate for open MRI. The design uses Fourier-series representations and Tikhonov regularization, with objectives such as field accuracy, power minimization, and optional shielding. Streamlines of the optimized current density provide implementable winding paths.
