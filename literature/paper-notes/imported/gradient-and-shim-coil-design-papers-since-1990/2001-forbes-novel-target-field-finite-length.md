## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper introduces a target-field method for genuinely finite-length magnetic resonance shim and gradient coils. A cylindrical target region can be placed asymmetrically inside a finite coil, and a desired field is specified on its surface. A regularized minimization produces the surface current density for zonal fields such as linear, quadratic, and cubic components.
