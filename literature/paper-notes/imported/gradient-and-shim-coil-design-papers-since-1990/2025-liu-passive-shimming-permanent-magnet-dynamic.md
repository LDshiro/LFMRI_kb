## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper proposes a passive-shimming optimization method for permanent-magnet MRI based on a dynamic target magnetic field. Instead of fixing all target values rigidly, the approach updates target field values to enlarge the feasible solution space. An optimization model and revised simplex-style solution are used to place correction pieces more effectively.
