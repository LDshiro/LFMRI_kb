## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper develops an inverse design method for transverse gradient coils on toroidal surfaces. The optimization combines field-error control with regularization terms related to power and practical winding behavior. It explores unshielded and shielded designs for whole-body and head-scale MRI.
