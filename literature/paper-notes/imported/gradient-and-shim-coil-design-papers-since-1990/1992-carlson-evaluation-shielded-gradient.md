## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This work presents design and experimental evaluation methods for actively shielded gradient coils. It compares predicted and measured coil behavior and addresses the practical tradeoffs among gradient linearity, efficiency, inductance, and external-field suppression. The paper is one of the early post-1990 references that connects shielded-gradient theory with implementable MRI hardware.
