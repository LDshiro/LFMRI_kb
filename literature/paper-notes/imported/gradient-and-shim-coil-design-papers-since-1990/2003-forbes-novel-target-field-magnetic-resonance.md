## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The third paper in the series adds active shielding to finite-length zonal and tesseral shim-coil design. Primary and shield winding patterns are designed so the desired target field is generated inside the target region while the field outside the shield is suppressed. The approach extends the finite-length target-field method to shielded shim hardware.
