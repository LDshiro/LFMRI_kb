## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

This paper formulates active magnetic screening for gradient coils. It describes how a second, actively driven coil can cancel external fringe fields so that switching gradients are decoupled from surrounding conducting structures, reducing eddy-current artifacts. The authors discuss design principles for screened coils and the construction issues that arise when the primary and shield systems must be co-optimized.
