## Imported abstract (EN, paraphrased)

The authors derive biplanar active shim-coil fields from scalar and vector potential formulations and include sine and cosine harmonic components. They design and fabricate nine biplanar shim coils for a permanent NMR analyzer. Experimental shimming demonstrates that the developed harmonic-field design procedure is practical.
